Who am I

Hi, my name is Jet and I am a software engineer based in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Despite the fact that I choose to dedicate my career to software engineering, I’ve always been dreaming about becoming a teacher ever since I was a kid. Teaching others gives me a sense of accomplishment that always fascinate me.

So, here I am ;)

I am not a smart guy, I always had to spend twice as much time as others did to reach the same level. I know how terrible it feels when not being able to understand a shit after reading several posts on Stack Overflow. So, at this website, I try to explain things in a way that everyone can easily understand, including any dumbass like me.

The content at this website will revolve around programming basics and tricks I’ve learned in my career. I hope what I write can provide some help to you, cheers.
